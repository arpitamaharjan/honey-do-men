About Us

Serving Homeowners In Putnam & Westchester Counties

Darrell Babbon

President And CEO Of Honey-Do-Men

Started in 1997 in Yorktown, New York, Honey-Do-Men aims to give homeowners in the area all-encompassing remodeling services. We are a one-of-a-kind service provider that offers all types of maintenance, rebuild, repair, and remodeling work for home exteriors and interiors. Whether you are searching for a reliable service provider to do a complete renovation of your home or just replace the windows, seal the driveway, or repair the roof, Honey-Do-Men is the right place to go.

Besides doing repairs, maintenance, and remodels, Honey-Do-Men also makes sure that every customer receives an excellent experience. From the second you reach out to us to even after the project has finished, you will enjoy an exceptional level of customer care and service that is uncommonly encountered in the home improvement industry. For instance, we take pride in punctuality, provide quotes for free, send reminders, and do follow-ups. If you have an issue with our work, we will come back to improve it to your satisfaction.
